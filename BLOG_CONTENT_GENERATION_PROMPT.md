## Content Guidelines

### Creative Freedom
- **Design beautiful layouts** - Use colors, backgrounds, borders, and spacing creatively
- **Create visual hierarchy** - Make important information stand out
- **Use styled containers** - Cards, boxes, highlights for different content types
- **Add personality** - Use emojis, icons, varied styling to engage readers
- **Think like a designer** - Balance aesthetics with readability
- **Make it scannable** - Use visual breaks, headings, and styled sections

### Length & Structure
- Aim for 1500-3000 words for depth and value
- Use clear visual hierarchy with styled headings
- Break content into digestible sections with visual variety
- Include different styled elements throughout (not just plain text)

### Tone
- Inspirational yet practical
- Evidence-based but accessible  
- Empowering and motivational
- Clear and engaging

### Content Elements to Include
- **Styled quote blocks** for the main quote and supporting quotes
- **Story/example boxes** with distinct backgrounds or borders
- **Insight/key point callouts** that stand out visually
- **Action steps** in visually organized lists or boxes
- **Statistics/research** in highlighted containers
- **Key takeaways section** with prominent styling
- **Visual variety** - Mix plain paragraphs with styled containers

### Writing Style
- Use short paragraphs (2-4 sentences) for readability
- Include concrete examples and stories in styled boxes
- Balance inspiration with practicality
- Avoid jargon - keep language accessible
- Write in second person ("you") to engage readers
- Use varied formatting to maintain visual interest

## Design Principles

### Color Usage
- Use subtle backgrounds (grays, light blues, yellows, greens) for different sections
- Keep text colors readable (dark on light backgrounds)
- Use accent colors (blues, purples) for borders and highlights
- Maintain consistency in color choices throughout

### Spacing
- Generous margins between major sections (2-2.5rem)
- Comfortable padding inside containers (1.5rem)
- Proper line height for readability (1.6-1.8)
- Breathing room around all elements

### Typography
- Clear hierarchy: h2 (1.875rem), h3 (1.25-1.5rem), p (1rem)
- Bold for emphasis and section labels
- Italic for quotes
- Varied sizes to create interest
