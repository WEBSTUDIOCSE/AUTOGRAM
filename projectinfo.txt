InstaAI Generator is an AI-powered Instagram automation platform that enables users to generate unique images using text prompts and automatically post them to Instagram. The application uses Google's Gemini AI for image generation and integrates with Instagram Graph API for seamless content publishing.

ğŸ’¡ Core Concept
Transform text descriptions into Instagram-ready images with a single click. Users describe what they want, AI generates it, and the system posts it directly to Instagram - all within one streamlined workflow.
